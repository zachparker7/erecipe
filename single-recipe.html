Italian Meatballs

by: vinny bagadonuts

Every good cook needs to have a good meatball recipe in their repertoire. In Italian families, their meatball recipe is usually passed down from generation to generation, and it's usually a close guarded secret. This recipe is my families recipe, and it's not so secret, but it will produce melt in your mouth recipe worthy of any pasta dish. Enjoy!

ingredients

1 lb. ground pork/veal/beef mix

2 eggs

3/4 cup italian breadcrumbs

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 cup parmesan cheese

3 tbsp. garlic powder

2 tsp. salt

1 tsp. ground black pepper

1/4 cup parsley chopped

1 tsp. oregano

preparation

1. In a large bowl, combine the meat, bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, milk, garlic powder, salt, pepper, parsley, and oregano, and work thoroughly until all ingredients are mixed.

2. Roll meat mixture into balls according to the size you'd like.

3. In a frying pan, heat up the olive oil until hot, and heat meatballs until they are cooked throughout, turning every few minutes.
